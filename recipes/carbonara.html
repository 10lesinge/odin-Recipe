Carbonara

We will cover the key points to make a proper Carbonara

Ingredients

  1. Spaghetti
  2. Guanciale
  3. Parmigiano
  4. Pecorino
  5. Eggs
  6. Garlic

Steps

  1. First cut you Guanciale in small pieces
  2. Bring water to boil and toss pasta
  3. Toss Guanciale and meanwhile beat 3 egg yolks with some grated Parmigiano and Pecorino
  4. Toss the pasta in the Guanciale pan with the egg & cheese mixture, add pasta water by the spoon until it's saucy
