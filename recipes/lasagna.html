Lasagna

How to make a Lasagna ?

We will cover the key points to make a proper lasagna

Ingredients

  • Grounded Beef
  • Tomato Puree
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Ricotta
  • Parmigiano
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Pasta Sheets

Steps

  1. Chop onions and carrots then toss in a pan with some olive oil until onions get soft
  2. Toss the ground beef until slightly cooked
  3. Add the tomato puree and let simmer down a bit while moving the mixture
  4. In a separate SaucePan, toss 50g of butter until it melts
  5. Add one spoon of flour and stir it well with the butter to obtain an homogen mixture
  6. Add 20cl of milk and stir well until the Bechamel is done (when it sticks to the spoon)
