Tartiflette

We will cover the key points to make a proper Tartiflette

Ingredients

  • Reblochon
  • Potato
  • Bacon
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • White wine
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. First put water to boil and add the potato
  2. Chop onions and bacon then add to a pan alongside garlic with hot oil
  3. After a few minutes toss the white wine in the pan
  4. Once cooked, cut your potato and display in an oven plate
  5. Toss your bacon, onions and garlic and stir with the Potato
  6. cut your Reblochon in half and place on top of the plate, face down
